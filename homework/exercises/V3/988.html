V3 - Spanning sets (ver. 988)

Explain why the vectors \(\left[\begin{array}{r} -5 \\ -3 \\ 3 \\ 4 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} -5 \\ 1 \\ 3 \\ -3 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} -4 \\ 0 \\ 0 \\ 3 \end{array}\right] , \text{ and } \left[\begin{array}{r} 29 \\ 3 \\ -15 \\ -2 \end{array}\right]\) span or don't span \(\mathbb{R}^4\).

Answer.

The vectors \(\left[\begin{array}{r} -5 \\ -3 \\ 3 \\ 4 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} -5 \\ 1 \\ 3 \\ -3 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} -4 \\ 0 \\ 0 \\ 3 \end{array}\right] , \text{ and } \left[\begin{array}{r} 29 \\ 3 \\ -15 \\ -2 \end{array}\right]\) do not span \(\mathbb{R}^4\).
