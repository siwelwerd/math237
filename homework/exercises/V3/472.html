V3 - Spanning sets (ver. 472)

Explain why the vectors \(\left[\begin{array}{r} -5 \\ 2 \\ 2 \\ 4 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 1 \\ -2 \\ -2 \\ 3 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} -9 \\ 2 \\ 2 \\ 11 \end{array}\right] , \text{ and } \left[\begin{array}{r} 25 \\ -10 \\ -10 \\ -20 \end{array}\right]\) span or don't span \(\mathbb{R}^4\).

Answer.

The vectors \(\left[\begin{array}{r} -5 \\ 2 \\ 2 \\ 4 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 1 \\ -2 \\ -2 \\ 3 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} -9 \\ 2 \\ 2 \\ 11 \end{array}\right] , \text{ and } \left[\begin{array}{r} 25 \\ -10 \\ -10 \\ -20 \end{array}\right]\) do not span \(\mathbb{R}^4\).
