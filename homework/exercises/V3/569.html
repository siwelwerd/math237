V3 - Spanning sets (ver. 569)

Explain why the vectors \(\left[\begin{array}{r} 1 \\ -5 \\ -1 \\ -5 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} -2 \\ -3 \\ -3 \\ -1 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 8 \\ -1 \\ 7 \\ -7 \end{array}\right] , \text{ and } \left[\begin{array}{r} -19 \\ 17 \\ -11 \\ 29 \end{array}\right]\) span or don't span \(\mathbb{R}^4\).

Answer.

The vectors \(\left[\begin{array}{r} 1 \\ -5 \\ -1 \\ -5 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} -2 \\ -3 \\ -3 \\ -1 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 8 \\ -1 \\ 7 \\ -7 \end{array}\right] , \text{ and } \left[\begin{array}{r} -19 \\ 17 \\ -11 \\ 29 \end{array}\right]\) do not span \(\mathbb{R}^4\).
