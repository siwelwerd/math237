V3 - Spanning sets (ver. 788)

Explain why the vectors \(\left[\begin{array}{r} -2 \\ -5 \\ 2 \\ -5 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 0 \\ 1 \\ 4 \\ -3 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} -5 \\ 4 \\ -4 \\ -3 \end{array}\right] , \text{ and } \left[\begin{array}{r} -8 \\ 11 \\ -18 \\ 5 \end{array}\right]\) span or don't span \(\mathbb{R}^4\).

Answer.

The vectors \(\left[\begin{array}{r} -2 \\ -5 \\ 2 \\ -5 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 0 \\ 1 \\ 4 \\ -3 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} -5 \\ 4 \\ -4 \\ -3 \end{array}\right] , \text{ and } \left[\begin{array}{r} -8 \\ 11 \\ -18 \\ 5 \end{array}\right]\) do not span \(\mathbb{R}^4\).
