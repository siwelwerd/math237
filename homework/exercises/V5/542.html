V5 - Linear Independence (ver. 542)

Explain why the vectors \(\left[\begin{array}{r} -3 \\ -4 \\ 3 \\ -3 \\ -4 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} -3 \\ 3 \\ -3 \\ -5 \\ 1 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} -4 \\ 4 \\ 5 \\ 4 \\ 5 \end{array}\right] , \text{ and } \left[\begin{array}{r} 3 \\ -17 \\ 15 \\ 9 \\ -11 \end{array}\right]\) are linearly dependent or linearly independent.

Answer.

The vectors \(\left[\begin{array}{r} -3 \\ -4 \\ 3 \\ -3 \\ -4 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} -3 \\ 3 \\ -3 \\ -5 \\ 1 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} -4 \\ 4 \\ 5 \\ 4 \\ 5 \end{array}\right] , \text{ and } \left[\begin{array}{r} 3 \\ -17 \\ 15 \\ 9 \\ -11 \end{array}\right]\) are linearly dependent .
