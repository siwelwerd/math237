V5 - Linear Independence (ver. 12)

Explain why the vectors \(\left[\begin{array}{r} 1 \\ -1 \\ 3 \\ -2 \\ 4 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} -3 \\ 4 \\ 2 \\ 4 \\ 2 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 7 \\ -9 \\ -1 \\ -10 \\ 0 \end{array}\right] , \text{ and } \left[\begin{array}{r} 12 \\ -21 \\ -11 \\ -14 \\ -12 \end{array}\right]\) are linearly dependent or linearly independent.

Answer.

The vectors \(\left[\begin{array}{r} 1 \\ -1 \\ 3 \\ -2 \\ 4 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} -3 \\ 4 \\ 2 \\ 4 \\ 2 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 7 \\ -9 \\ -1 \\ -10 \\ 0 \end{array}\right] , \text{ and } \left[\begin{array}{r} 12 \\ -21 \\ -11 \\ -14 \\ -12 \end{array}\right]\) are linearly dependent .
