V5 - Linear Independence (ver. 261)

Explain why the vectors \(\left[\begin{array}{r} 0 \\ -5 \\ 3 \\ -5 \\ -6 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 2 \\ -4 \\ 1 \\ -2 \\ 5 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 0 \\ 2 \\ -6 \\ -4 \\ -2 \end{array}\right] , \text{ and } \left[\begin{array}{r} 2 \\ 6 \\ -5 \\ 8 \\ 17 \end{array}\right]\) are linearly dependent or linearly independent.

Answer.

The vectors \(\left[\begin{array}{r} 0 \\ -5 \\ 3 \\ -5 \\ -6 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 2 \\ -4 \\ 1 \\ -2 \\ 5 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 0 \\ 2 \\ -6 \\ -4 \\ -2 \end{array}\right] , \text{ and } \left[\begin{array}{r} 2 \\ 6 \\ -5 \\ 8 \\ 17 \end{array}\right]\) are linearly dependent .
