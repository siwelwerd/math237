V5 - Linear Independence (ver. 184)

Explain why the vectors \(\left[\begin{array}{r} -3 \\ 1 \\ 2 \\ 5 \\ -3 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} -1 \\ -2 \\ 1 \\ 1 \\ 0 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} -2 \\ -2 \\ 2 \\ 1 \\ 3 \end{array}\right] , \text{ and } \left[\begin{array}{r} 5 \\ 0 \\ -3 \\ -11 \\ 12 \end{array}\right]\) are linearly dependent or linearly independent.

Answer.

The vectors \(\left[\begin{array}{r} -3 \\ 1 \\ 2 \\ 5 \\ -3 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} -1 \\ -2 \\ 1 \\ 1 \\ 0 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} -2 \\ -2 \\ 2 \\ 1 \\ 3 \end{array}\right] , \text{ and } \left[\begin{array}{r} 5 \\ 0 \\ -3 \\ -11 \\ 12 \end{array}\right]\) are linearly dependent .
