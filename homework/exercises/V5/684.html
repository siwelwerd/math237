V5 - Linear Independence (ver. 684)

Explain why the vectors \(\left[\begin{array}{r} -1 \\ 4 \\ 5 \\ -6 \\ -2 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} -3 \\ -4 \\ -3 \\ -1 \\ -5 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 10 \\ 8 \\ 4 \\ 9 \\ 17 \end{array}\right] , \text{ and } \left[\begin{array}{r} -11 \\ 2 \\ -3 \\ -11 \\ -17 \end{array}\right]\) are linearly dependent or linearly independent.

Answer.

The vectors \(\left[\begin{array}{r} -1 \\ 4 \\ 5 \\ -6 \\ -2 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} -3 \\ -4 \\ -3 \\ -1 \\ -5 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 10 \\ 8 \\ 4 \\ 9 \\ 17 \end{array}\right] , \text{ and } \left[\begin{array}{r} -11 \\ 2 \\ -3 \\ -11 \\ -17 \end{array}\right]\) are linearly dependent .
