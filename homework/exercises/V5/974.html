V5 - Linear Independence (ver. 974)

Explain why the vectors \(\left[\begin{array}{r} 2 \\ 2 \\ 4 \\ -4 \\ 4 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 0 \\ 2 \\ 1 \\ 5 \\ -6 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} -2 \\ -3 \\ 5 \\ 4 \\ 5 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 4 \\ 4 \\ 8 \\ -8 \\ 8 \end{array}\right] , \text{ and } \left[\begin{array}{r} 0 \\ -5 \\ 7 \\ -10 \\ 21 \end{array}\right]\) are linearly dependent or linearly independent.

Answer.

The vectors \(\left[\begin{array}{r} 2 \\ 2 \\ 4 \\ -4 \\ 4 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 0 \\ 2 \\ 1 \\ 5 \\ -6 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} -2 \\ -3 \\ 5 \\ 4 \\ 5 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 4 \\ 4 \\ 8 \\ -8 \\ 8 \end{array}\right] , \text{ and } \left[\begin{array}{r} 0 \\ -5 \\ 7 \\ -10 \\ 21 \end{array}\right]\) are linearly dependent .
