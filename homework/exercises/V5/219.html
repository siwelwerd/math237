V5 - Linear Independence (ver. 219)

Explain why the vectors \(\left[\begin{array}{r} -3 \\ -6 \\ -1 \\ 2 \\ -3 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 5 \\ 5 \\ -4 \\ 5 \\ -4 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 3 \\ -6 \\ -4 \\ 1 \\ 3 \end{array}\right] , \text{ and } \left[\begin{array}{r} 1 \\ 4 \\ -6 \\ 13 \\ -17 \end{array}\right]\) are linearly dependent or linearly independent.

Answer.

The vectors \(\left[\begin{array}{r} -3 \\ -6 \\ -1 \\ 2 \\ -3 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 5 \\ 5 \\ -4 \\ 5 \\ -4 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 3 \\ -6 \\ -4 \\ 1 \\ 3 \end{array}\right] , \text{ and } \left[\begin{array}{r} 1 \\ 4 \\ -6 \\ 13 \\ -17 \end{array}\right]\) are linearly dependent .
