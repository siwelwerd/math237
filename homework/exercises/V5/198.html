V5 - Linear Independence (ver. 198)

Explain why the vectors \(\left[\begin{array}{r} 2 \\ 0 \\ 3 \\ -3 \\ -6 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 3 \\ 4 \\ -5 \\ 4 \\ -3 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 0 \\ -1 \\ -4 \\ -4 \\ -1 \end{array}\right] , \text{ and } \left[\begin{array}{r} 5 \\ 6 \\ 6 \\ 9 \\ -7 \end{array}\right]\) are linearly dependent or linearly independent.

Answer.

The vectors \(\left[\begin{array}{r} 2 \\ 0 \\ 3 \\ -3 \\ -6 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 3 \\ 4 \\ -5 \\ 4 \\ -3 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 0 \\ -1 \\ -4 \\ -4 \\ -1 \end{array}\right] , \text{ and } \left[\begin{array}{r} 5 \\ 6 \\ 6 \\ 9 \\ -7 \end{array}\right]\) are linearly dependent .
