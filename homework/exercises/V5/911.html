V5 - Linear Independence (ver. 911)

Explain why the vectors \(\left[\begin{array}{r} 5 \\ -2 \\ 2 \\ 0 \\ -6 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} -6 \\ -6 \\ 3 \\ 0 \\ -1 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 2 \\ -4 \\ -6 \\ 2 \\ -3 \end{array}\right] , \text{ and } \left[\begin{array}{r} -33 \\ 6 \\ 18 \\ -6 \\ 25 \end{array}\right]\) are linearly dependent or linearly independent.

Answer.

The vectors \(\left[\begin{array}{r} 5 \\ -2 \\ 2 \\ 0 \\ -6 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} -6 \\ -6 \\ 3 \\ 0 \\ -1 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 2 \\ -4 \\ -6 \\ 2 \\ -3 \end{array}\right] , \text{ and } \left[\begin{array}{r} -33 \\ 6 \\ 18 \\ -6 \\ 25 \end{array}\right]\) are linearly dependent .
