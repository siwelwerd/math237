V5 - Linear Independence (ver. 139)

Explain why the vectors \(\left[\begin{array}{r} 5 \\ 1 \\ -5 \\ 4 \\ -2 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 5 \\ 1 \\ 0 \\ -2 \\ -6 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 3 \\ -4 \\ -4 \\ -6 \\ -5 \end{array}\right] , \text{ and } \left[\begin{array}{r} 16 \\ -6 \\ -18 \\ -4 \\ -14 \end{array}\right]\) are linearly dependent or linearly independent.

Answer.

The vectors \(\left[\begin{array}{r} 5 \\ 1 \\ -5 \\ 4 \\ -2 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 5 \\ 1 \\ 0 \\ -2 \\ -6 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 3 \\ -4 \\ -4 \\ -6 \\ -5 \end{array}\right] , \text{ and } \left[\begin{array}{r} 16 \\ -6 \\ -18 \\ -4 \\ -14 \end{array}\right]\) are linearly dependent .
