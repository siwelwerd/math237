V5 - Linear Independence (ver. 333)

Explain why the vectors \(\left[\begin{array}{r} 1 \\ 2 \\ 0 \\ -5 \\ 5 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 5 \\ -6 \\ -2 \\ -3 \\ -3 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} -8 \\ 0 \\ 2 \\ 18 \\ -12 \end{array}\right] , \text{ and } \left[\begin{array}{r} 7 \\ -8 \\ -5 \\ -9 \\ -7 \end{array}\right]\) are linearly dependent or linearly independent.

Answer.

The vectors \(\left[\begin{array}{r} 1 \\ 2 \\ 0 \\ -5 \\ 5 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 5 \\ -6 \\ -2 \\ -3 \\ -3 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} -8 \\ 0 \\ 2 \\ 18 \\ -12 \end{array}\right] , \text{ and } \left[\begin{array}{r} 7 \\ -8 \\ -5 \\ -9 \\ -7 \end{array}\right]\) are linearly dependent .
