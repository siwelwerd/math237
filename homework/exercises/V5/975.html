V5 - Linear Independence (ver. 975)

Explain why the vectors \(\left[\begin{array}{r} 4 \\ -6 \\ -3 \\ -1 \\ 4 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 1 \\ -5 \\ -5 \\ 1 \\ 5 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} -6 \\ 16 \\ 13 \\ -1 \\ -14 \end{array}\right] , \text{ and } \left[\begin{array}{r} 9 \\ -17 \\ -11 \\ -1 \\ 13 \end{array}\right]\) are linearly dependent or linearly independent.

Answer.

The vectors \(\left[\begin{array}{r} 4 \\ -6 \\ -3 \\ -1 \\ 4 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 1 \\ -5 \\ -5 \\ 1 \\ 5 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} -6 \\ 16 \\ 13 \\ -1 \\ -14 \end{array}\right] , \text{ and } \left[\begin{array}{r} 9 \\ -17 \\ -11 \\ -1 \\ 13 \end{array}\right]\) are linearly dependent .
