V5 - Linear Independence (ver. 709)

Explain why the vectors \(\left[\begin{array}{r} -6 \\ -6 \\ 4 \\ 3 \\ 4 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} -4 \\ 3 \\ 1 \\ 5 \\ 0 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 26 \\ 12 \\ -14 \\ -19 \\ -12 \end{array}\right] , \text{ and } \left[\begin{array}{r} -26 \\ 4 \\ 16 \\ 10 \\ 0 \end{array}\right]\) are linearly dependent or linearly independent.

Answer.

The vectors \(\left[\begin{array}{r} -6 \\ -6 \\ 4 \\ 3 \\ 4 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} -4 \\ 3 \\ 1 \\ 5 \\ 0 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 26 \\ 12 \\ -14 \\ -19 \\ -12 \end{array}\right] , \text{ and } \left[\begin{array}{r} -26 \\ 4 \\ 16 \\ 10 \\ 0 \end{array}\right]\) are linearly dependent .
