V5 - Linear Independence (ver. 168)

Explain why the vectors \(\left[\begin{array}{r} 2 \\ -1 \\ -6 \\ 3 \\ 1 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 5 \\ -3 \\ -6 \\ 3 \\ 4 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 9 \\ -5 \\ -18 \\ 9 \\ 6 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} -2 \\ 3 \\ -1 \\ 3 \\ -2 \end{array}\right] , \text{ and } \left[\begin{array}{r} -17 \\ 6 \\ 24 \\ -12 \\ -7 \end{array}\right]\) are linearly dependent or linearly independent.

Answer.

The vectors \(\left[\begin{array}{r} 2 \\ -1 \\ -6 \\ 3 \\ 1 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 5 \\ -3 \\ -6 \\ 3 \\ 4 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 9 \\ -5 \\ -18 \\ 9 \\ 6 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} -2 \\ 3 \\ -1 \\ 3 \\ -2 \end{array}\right] , \text{ and } \left[\begin{array}{r} -17 \\ 6 \\ 24 \\ -12 \\ -7 \end{array}\right]\) are linearly dependent .
