V5 - Linear Independence (ver. 559)

Explain why the vectors \(\left[\begin{array}{r} -1 \\ 4 \\ -3 \\ 2 \\ 4 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} -1 \\ 0 \\ -5 \\ -5 \\ -4 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 0 \\ -5 \\ 1 \\ 1 \\ 5 \end{array}\right] , \text{ and } \left[\begin{array}{r} 1 \\ 13 \\ 8 \\ 18 \\ 15 \end{array}\right]\) are linearly dependent or linearly independent.

Answer.

The vectors \(\left[\begin{array}{r} -1 \\ 4 \\ -3 \\ 2 \\ 4 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} -1 \\ 0 \\ -5 \\ -5 \\ -4 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 0 \\ -5 \\ 1 \\ 1 \\ 5 \end{array}\right] , \text{ and } \left[\begin{array}{r} 1 \\ 13 \\ 8 \\ 18 \\ 15 \end{array}\right]\) are linearly dependent .
