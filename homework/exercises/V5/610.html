V5 - Linear Independence (ver. 610)

Explain why the vectors \(\left[\begin{array}{r} -2 \\ 2 \\ 4 \\ -6 \\ -6 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 2 \\ 0 \\ 1 \\ 5 \\ -1 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 5 \\ -1 \\ -5 \\ -6 \\ 5 \end{array}\right] , \text{ and } \left[\begin{array}{r} -11 \\ -1 \\ 7 \\ 30 \\ -3 \end{array}\right]\) are linearly dependent or linearly independent.

Answer.

The vectors \(\left[\begin{array}{r} -2 \\ 2 \\ 4 \\ -6 \\ -6 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 2 \\ 0 \\ 1 \\ 5 \\ -1 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 5 \\ -1 \\ -5 \\ -6 \\ 5 \end{array}\right] , \text{ and } \left[\begin{array}{r} -11 \\ -1 \\ 7 \\ 30 \\ -3 \end{array}\right]\) are linearly dependent .
