V5 - Linear Independence (ver. 801)

Explain why the vectors \(\left[\begin{array}{r} -4 \\ -5 \\ 0 \\ 5 \\ 5 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 3 \\ -2 \\ -3 \\ -4 \\ 0 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 7 \\ 3 \\ -3 \\ -9 \\ -5 \end{array}\right] , \text{ and } \left[\begin{array}{r} 3 \\ -14 \\ -11 \\ 18 \\ 22 \end{array}\right]\) are linearly dependent or linearly independent.

Answer.

The vectors \(\left[\begin{array}{r} -4 \\ -5 \\ 0 \\ 5 \\ 5 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 3 \\ -2 \\ -3 \\ -4 \\ 0 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 7 \\ 3 \\ -3 \\ -9 \\ -5 \end{array}\right] , \text{ and } \left[\begin{array}{r} 3 \\ -14 \\ -11 \\ 18 \\ 22 \end{array}\right]\) are linearly dependent .
