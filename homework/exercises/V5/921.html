V5 - Linear Independence (ver. 921)

Explain why the vectors \(\left[\begin{array}{r} 2 \\ -5 \\ 4 \\ 2 \\ -1 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} -6 \\ -5 \\ -1 \\ -4 \\ -4 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} -5 \\ -6 \\ 0 \\ -1 \\ 1 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 30 \\ 22 \\ 7 \\ 16 \\ 9 \end{array}\right] , \text{ and } \left[\begin{array}{r} 18 \\ 15 \\ 3 \\ 12 \\ 12 \end{array}\right]\) are linearly dependent or linearly independent.

Answer.

The vectors \(\left[\begin{array}{r} 2 \\ -5 \\ 4 \\ 2 \\ -1 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} -6 \\ -5 \\ -1 \\ -4 \\ -4 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} -5 \\ -6 \\ 0 \\ -1 \\ 1 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 30 \\ 22 \\ 7 \\ 16 \\ 9 \end{array}\right] , \text{ and } \left[\begin{array}{r} 18 \\ 15 \\ 3 \\ 12 \\ 12 \end{array}\right]\) are linearly dependent .
