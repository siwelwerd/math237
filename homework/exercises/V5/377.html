V5 - Linear Independence (ver. 377)

Explain why the vectors \(\left[\begin{array}{r} -6 \\ 4 \\ 3 \\ -2 \\ 1 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} -6 \\ -5 \\ -3 \\ 3 \\ -2 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 30 \\ 7 \\ 3 \\ -5 \\ 4 \end{array}\right] , \text{ and } \left[\begin{array}{r} 24 \\ -8 \\ -7 \\ 10 \\ 7 \end{array}\right]\) are linearly dependent or linearly independent.

Answer.

The vectors \(\left[\begin{array}{r} -6 \\ 4 \\ 3 \\ -2 \\ 1 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} -6 \\ -5 \\ -3 \\ 3 \\ -2 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 30 \\ 7 \\ 3 \\ -5 \\ 4 \end{array}\right] , \text{ and } \left[\begin{array}{r} 24 \\ -8 \\ -7 \\ 10 \\ 7 \end{array}\right]\) are linearly dependent .
