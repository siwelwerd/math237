V5 - Linear Independence (ver. 847)

Explain why the vectors \(\left[\begin{array}{r} 1 \\ 1 \\ -1 \\ 0 \\ 3 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 1 \\ 3 \\ -6 \\ -6 \\ -4 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} -2 \\ -8 \\ 17 \\ 18 \\ 15 \end{array}\right] , \text{ and } \left[\begin{array}{r} 2 \\ 6 \\ -12 \\ -12 \\ -8 \end{array}\right]\) are linearly dependent or linearly independent.

Answer.

The vectors \(\left[\begin{array}{r} 1 \\ 1 \\ -1 \\ 0 \\ 3 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 1 \\ 3 \\ -6 \\ -6 \\ -4 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} -2 \\ -8 \\ 17 \\ 18 \\ 15 \end{array}\right] , \text{ and } \left[\begin{array}{r} 2 \\ 6 \\ -12 \\ -12 \\ -8 \end{array}\right]\) are linearly dependent .
