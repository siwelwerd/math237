V5 - Linear Independence (ver. 336)

Explain why the vectors \(\left[\begin{array}{r} -6 \\ 4 \\ 3 \\ 5 \\ 4 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 3 \\ -4 \\ -5 \\ 1 \\ -5 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} -2 \\ 3 \\ 4 \\ 5 \\ 3 \end{array}\right] , \text{ and } \left[\begin{array}{r} -12 \\ 12 \\ 13 \\ 3 \\ 14 \end{array}\right]\) are linearly dependent or linearly independent.

Answer.

The vectors \(\left[\begin{array}{r} -6 \\ 4 \\ 3 \\ 5 \\ 4 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 3 \\ -4 \\ -5 \\ 1 \\ -5 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} -2 \\ 3 \\ 4 \\ 5 \\ 3 \end{array}\right] , \text{ and } \left[\begin{array}{r} -12 \\ 12 \\ 13 \\ 3 \\ 14 \end{array}\right]\) are linearly dependent .
