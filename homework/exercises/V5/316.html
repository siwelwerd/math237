V5 - Linear Independence (ver. 316)

Explain why the vectors \(\left[\begin{array}{r} 0 \\ 4 \\ -3 \\ -3 \\ -6 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} -4 \\ 0 \\ 4 \\ 2 \\ 3 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 0 \\ -1 \\ -1 \\ -6 \\ -1 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} -1 \\ 5 \\ 4 \\ -3 \\ -3 \end{array}\right] , \text{ and } \left[\begin{array}{r} 8 \\ -10 \\ 3 \\ 17 \\ 14 \end{array}\right]\) are linearly dependent or linearly independent.

Answer.

The vectors \(\left[\begin{array}{r} 0 \\ 4 \\ -3 \\ -3 \\ -6 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} -4 \\ 0 \\ 4 \\ 2 \\ 3 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 0 \\ -1 \\ -1 \\ -6 \\ -1 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} -1 \\ 5 \\ 4 \\ -3 \\ -3 \end{array}\right] , \text{ and } \left[\begin{array}{r} 8 \\ -10 \\ 3 \\ 17 \\ 14 \end{array}\right]\) are linearly dependent .
