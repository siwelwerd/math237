V5 - Linear Independence (ver. 820)

Explain why the vectors \(\left[\begin{array}{r} -2 \\ 5 \\ -5 \\ 4 \\ -3 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 5 \\ 1 \\ -6 \\ 5 \\ -5 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 10 \\ 2 \\ -12 \\ 10 \\ -10 \end{array}\right] , \text{ and } \left[\begin{array}{r} 6 \\ -3 \\ 2 \\ 24 \\ -4 \end{array}\right]\) are linearly dependent or linearly independent.

Answer.

The vectors \(\left[\begin{array}{r} -2 \\ 5 \\ -5 \\ 4 \\ -3 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 5 \\ 1 \\ -6 \\ 5 \\ -5 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 10 \\ 2 \\ -12 \\ 10 \\ -10 \end{array}\right] , \text{ and } \left[\begin{array}{r} 6 \\ -3 \\ 2 \\ 24 \\ -4 \end{array}\right]\) are linearly dependent .
