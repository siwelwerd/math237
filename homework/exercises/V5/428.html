V5 - Linear Independence (ver. 428)

Explain why the vectors \(\left[\begin{array}{r} 3 \\ 4 \\ -5 \\ -1 \\ 5 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 5 \\ -5 \\ 3 \\ 0 \\ -3 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 3 \\ 1 \\ 4 \\ -4 \\ -3 \end{array}\right] , \text{ and } \left[\begin{array}{r} 2 \\ -9 \\ 8 \\ 1 \\ -8 \end{array}\right]\) are linearly dependent or linearly independent.

Answer.

The vectors \(\left[\begin{array}{r} 3 \\ 4 \\ -5 \\ -1 \\ 5 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 5 \\ -5 \\ 3 \\ 0 \\ -3 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 3 \\ 1 \\ 4 \\ -4 \\ -3 \end{array}\right] , \text{ and } \left[\begin{array}{r} 2 \\ -9 \\ 8 \\ 1 \\ -8 \end{array}\right]\) are linearly dependent .
