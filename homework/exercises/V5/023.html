V5 - Linear Independence (ver. 23)

Explain why the vectors \(\left[\begin{array}{r} -5 \\ -5 \\ -2 \\ 4 \\ -5 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 0 \\ -6 \\ 1 \\ 4 \\ -3 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 0 \\ -12 \\ 2 \\ 8 \\ -6 \end{array}\right] , \text{ and } \left[\begin{array}{r} 17 \\ 23 \\ -15 \\ -18 \\ 23 \end{array}\right]\) are linearly dependent or linearly independent.

Answer.

The vectors \(\left[\begin{array}{r} -5 \\ -5 \\ -2 \\ 4 \\ -5 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 0 \\ -6 \\ 1 \\ 4 \\ -3 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 0 \\ -12 \\ 2 \\ 8 \\ -6 \end{array}\right] , \text{ and } \left[\begin{array}{r} 17 \\ 23 \\ -15 \\ -18 \\ 23 \end{array}\right]\) are linearly dependent .
