V5 - Linear Independence (ver. 573)

Explain why the vectors \(\left[\begin{array}{r} -5 \\ -6 \\ -2 \\ -4 \\ -1 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 3 \\ 5 \\ -4 \\ 0 \\ -2 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} -1 \\ 2 \\ -1 \\ 0 \\ -5 \end{array}\right] , \text{ and } \left[\begin{array}{r} 21 \\ 28 \\ -2 \\ 12 \\ -1 \end{array}\right]\) are linearly dependent or linearly independent.

Answer.

The vectors \(\left[\begin{array}{r} -5 \\ -6 \\ -2 \\ -4 \\ -1 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 3 \\ 5 \\ -4 \\ 0 \\ -2 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} -1 \\ 2 \\ -1 \\ 0 \\ -5 \end{array}\right] , \text{ and } \left[\begin{array}{r} 21 \\ 28 \\ -2 \\ 12 \\ -1 \end{array}\right]\) are linearly dependent .
