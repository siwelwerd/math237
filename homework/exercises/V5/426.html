V5 - Linear Independence (ver. 426)

Explain why the vectors \(\left[\begin{array}{r} 3 \\ 1 \\ -6 \\ -1 \\ 5 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 3 \\ 3 \\ 0 \\ -6 \\ -4 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 6 \\ 6 \\ 0 \\ -12 \\ -8 \end{array}\right] , \text{ and } \left[\begin{array}{r} 0 \\ 3 \\ 18 \\ 21 \\ 23 \end{array}\right]\) are linearly dependent or linearly independent.

Answer.

The vectors \(\left[\begin{array}{r} 3 \\ 1 \\ -6 \\ -1 \\ 5 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 3 \\ 3 \\ 0 \\ -6 \\ -4 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 6 \\ 6 \\ 0 \\ -12 \\ -8 \end{array}\right] , \text{ and } \left[\begin{array}{r} 0 \\ 3 \\ 18 \\ 21 \\ 23 \end{array}\right]\) are linearly dependent .
