V5 - Linear Independence (ver. 562)

Explain why the vectors \(\left[\begin{array}{r} 1 \\ 5 \\ -6 \\ -4 \\ -4 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} -5 \\ 2 \\ -3 \\ 1 \\ 0 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 2 \\ -3 \\ -6 \\ -2 \\ 4 \end{array}\right] , \text{ and } \left[\begin{array}{r} 8 \\ -14 \\ 18 \\ 6 \\ 8 \end{array}\right]\) are linearly dependent or linearly independent.

Answer.

The vectors \(\left[\begin{array}{r} 1 \\ 5 \\ -6 \\ -4 \\ -4 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} -5 \\ 2 \\ -3 \\ 1 \\ 0 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 2 \\ -3 \\ -6 \\ -2 \\ 4 \end{array}\right] , \text{ and } \left[\begin{array}{r} 8 \\ -14 \\ 18 \\ 6 \\ 8 \end{array}\right]\) are linearly dependent .
