V5 - Linear Independence (ver. 86)

Explain why the vectors \(\left[\begin{array}{r} -6 \\ -3 \\ 1 \\ 3 \\ 3 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 3 \\ 2 \\ -6 \\ 2 \\ 5 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 6 \\ 4 \\ -12 \\ 4 \\ 10 \end{array}\right] , \text{ and } \left[\begin{array}{r} 19 \\ 7 \\ -13 \\ -11 \\ -10 \end{array}\right]\) are linearly dependent or linearly independent.

Answer.

The vectors \(\left[\begin{array}{r} -6 \\ -3 \\ 1 \\ 3 \\ 3 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 3 \\ 2 \\ -6 \\ 2 \\ 5 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 6 \\ 4 \\ -12 \\ 4 \\ 10 \end{array}\right] , \text{ and } \left[\begin{array}{r} 19 \\ 7 \\ -13 \\ -11 \\ -10 \end{array}\right]\) are linearly dependent .
