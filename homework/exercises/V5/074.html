V5 - Linear Independence (ver. 74)

Explain why the vectors \(\left[\begin{array}{r} 1 \\ 0 \\ -5 \\ 4 \\ -1 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 5 \\ -1 \\ 4 \\ -1 \\ 4 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} -6 \\ 0 \\ -4 \\ -2 \\ -6 \end{array}\right] , \text{ and } \left[\begin{array}{r} -8 \\ 1 \\ 11 \\ -11 \\ -1 \end{array}\right]\) are linearly dependent or linearly independent.

Answer.

The vectors \(\left[\begin{array}{r} 1 \\ 0 \\ -5 \\ 4 \\ -1 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 5 \\ -1 \\ 4 \\ -1 \\ 4 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} -6 \\ 0 \\ -4 \\ -2 \\ -6 \end{array}\right] , \text{ and } \left[\begin{array}{r} -8 \\ 1 \\ 11 \\ -11 \\ -1 \end{array}\right]\) are linearly dependent .
