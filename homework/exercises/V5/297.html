V5 - Linear Independence (ver. 297)

Explain why the vectors \(\left[\begin{array}{r} 5 \\ 0 \\ -1 \\ 5 \\ -4 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} -3 \\ -5 \\ 0 \\ 3 \\ 0 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 1 \\ 0 \\ -5 \\ -3 \\ -5 \end{array}\right] , \text{ and } \left[\begin{array}{r} 19 \\ 15 \\ -2 \\ 1 \\ -8 \end{array}\right]\) are linearly dependent or linearly independent.

Answer.

The vectors \(\left[\begin{array}{r} 5 \\ 0 \\ -1 \\ 5 \\ -4 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} -3 \\ -5 \\ 0 \\ 3 \\ 0 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 1 \\ 0 \\ -5 \\ -3 \\ -5 \end{array}\right] , \text{ and } \left[\begin{array}{r} 19 \\ 15 \\ -2 \\ 1 \\ -8 \end{array}\right]\) are linearly dependent .
