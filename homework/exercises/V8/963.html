Explain how to find the dimension of

\[W=\mathrm{span}\ \left\{\left[\begin{array}{r} -3 \\ -3 \\ 3 \\ 1 \\ 3 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} -4 \\ -3 \\ -2 \\ 3 \\ -2 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 12 \\ 12 \\ -12 \\ -4 \\ -12 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} -32 \\ -30 \\ 20 \\ 14 \\ 20 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} -45 \\ -45 \\ 45 \\ 15 \\ 45 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} -2 \\ -3 \\ 8 \\ -1 \\ 8 \end{array}\right]\right\}.\]

Answer.

The dimension of \(W\) is \(2\).
