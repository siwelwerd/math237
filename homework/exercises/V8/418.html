Explain how to find the dimension of

\[W=\mathrm{span}\ \left\{\left[\begin{array}{r} -4 \\ -4 \\ -4 \\ -2 \\ -3 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 0 \\ 1 \\ 2 \\ 0 \\ -1 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 8 \\ 8 \\ 8 \\ 4 \\ 6 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 0 \\ -1 \\ -2 \\ 0 \\ 1 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 3 \\ -2 \\ -2 \\ -4 \\ 1 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} -4 \\ -3 \\ -2 \\ -2 \\ -4 \end{array}\right]\right\}.\]

Answer.

The dimension of \(W\) is \(3\).
