V8 - Dimension (ver. 964)

Explain how to find the dimension of

\[W=\mathrm{span}\ \left\{\left[\begin{array}{r} -4 \\ 1 \\ -2 \\ -4 \\ 2 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} -1 \\ 2 \\ 1 \\ -1 \\ -2 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 5 \\ 4 \\ 7 \\ 5 \\ -10 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} -4 \\ -6 \\ -8 \\ -4 \\ 12 \end{array}\right]\right\}.\]

Answer.

The dimension of \(W\) is \(2\).
