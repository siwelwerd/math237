V8 - Dimension (ver. 335)

Explain how to find the dimension of

\[W=\mathrm{span}\ \left\{\left[\begin{array}{r} 1 \\ 3 \\ -2 \\ -3 \\ -3 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 0 \\ 2 \\ -2 \\ -4 \\ 2 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} -4 \\ 0 \\ -3 \\ -3 \\ 3 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} -3 \\ 7 \\ -9 \\ -14 \\ 4 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 15 \\ -17 \\ 28 \\ 43 \\ -23 \end{array}\right]\right\}.\]

Answer.

The dimension of \(W\) is \(3\).
