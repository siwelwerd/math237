Explain how to find the dimension of

\[W=\mathrm{span}\ \left\{\left[\begin{array}{r} -2 \\ -2 \\ -4 \\ 2 \\ -2 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 2 \\ -3 \\ -3 \\ -3 \\ -2 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} -2 \\ -1 \\ -1 \\ -4 \\ 3 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 2 \\ -8 \\ -10 \\ -4 \\ -6 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 0 \\ 24 \\ 32 \\ 11 \\ 15 \end{array}\right]\right\}.\]

Answer.

The dimension of \(W\) is \(3\).
