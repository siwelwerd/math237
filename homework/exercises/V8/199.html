Explain how to find the dimension of

\[W=\mathrm{span}\ \left\{\left[\begin{array}{r} -2 \\ -3 \\ 2 \\ -1 \\ -1 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 1 \\ -3 \\ -2 \\ -1 \\ 3 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 6 \\ -9 \\ -10 \\ -3 \\ 13 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} -6 \\ 27 \\ 14 \\ 9 \\ -23 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} -23 \\ 69 \\ 46 \\ 23 \\ -69 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 3 \\ 0 \\ -4 \\ 0 \\ 4 \end{array}\right]\right\}.\]

Answer.

The dimension of \(W\) is \(2\).
