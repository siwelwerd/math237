V8 - Dimension (ver. 917)

Explain how to find the dimension of

\[W=\mathrm{span}\ \left\{\left[\begin{array}{r} 1 \\ 1 \\ -1 \\ 2 \\ -4 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 2 \\ -3 \\ -2 \\ -1 \\ -4 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 1 \\ 0 \\ 0 \\ 1 \\ -3 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} -6 \\ 7 \\ 3 \\ 1 \\ 13 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} -8 \\ 18 \\ 7 \\ 10 \\ 11 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} -2 \\ -4 \\ 2 \\ -2 \\ -2 \end{array}\right]\right\}.\]

Answer.

The dimension of \(W\) is \(4\).
