V8 - Dimension (ver. 668)

Explain how to find the dimension of

\[W=\mathrm{span}\ \left\{\left[\begin{array}{r} -3 \\ 1 \\ 3 \\ -4 \\ 2 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} -1 \\ -4 \\ -4 \\ -3 \\ 0 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} -4 \\ 23 \\ 29 \\ 3 \\ 6 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 8 \\ -33 \\ -43 \\ -1 \\ -10 \end{array}\right]\right\}.\]

Answer.

The dimension of \(W\) is \(2\).
