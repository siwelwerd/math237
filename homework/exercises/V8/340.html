Explain how to find the dimension of

\[W=\mathrm{span}\ \left\{\left[\begin{array}{r} -4 \\ -3 \\ 1 \\ 2 \\ -3 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 0 \\ -2 \\ -4 \\ -2 \\ -4 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} -16 \\ -14 \\ 0 \\ 6 \\ -16 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} -20 \\ -23 \\ -11 \\ 2 \\ -31 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} -44 \\ -53 \\ -29 \\ 2 \\ -73 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 2 \\ 0 \\ -1 \\ 2 \\ 1 \end{array}\right]\right\}.\]

Answer.

The dimension of \(W\) is \(3\).
