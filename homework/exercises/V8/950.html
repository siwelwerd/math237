Explain how to find the dimension of

\[W=\mathrm{span}\ \left\{\left[\begin{array}{r} 1 \\ 0 \\ -4 \\ -2 \\ -3 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} -4 \\ 3 \\ 0 \\ -2 \\ 3 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 14 \\ -9 \\ -8 \\ 2 \\ -15 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} -24 \\ 15 \\ 16 \\ -2 \\ 27 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 44 \\ -27 \\ -32 \\ 2 \\ -51 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} -56 \\ 36 \\ 32 \\ -8 \\ 60 \end{array}\right]\right\}.\]

Answer.

The dimension of \(W\) is \(2\).
