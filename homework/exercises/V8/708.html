V8 - Dimension (ver. 708)

Explain how to find the dimension of

\[W=\mathrm{span}\ \left\{\left[\begin{array}{r} -3 \\ -4 \\ 1 \\ -3 \\ 0 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 2 \\ -1 \\ 0 \\ -2 \\ -1 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 0 \\ -3 \\ -3 \\ 3 \\ -3 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 5 \\ 8 \\ -9 \\ 19 \\ -4 \end{array}\right]\right\}.\]

Answer.

The dimension of \(W\) is \(3\).
