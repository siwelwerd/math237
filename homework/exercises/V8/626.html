V8 - Dimension (ver. 626)

Explain how to find the dimension of

\[W=\mathrm{span}\ \left\{\left[\begin{array}{r} 2 \\ 2 \\ -3 \\ -4 \\ -3 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} -2 \\ 3 \\ -3 \\ 0 \\ 3 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 10 \\ 0 \\ -3 \\ -12 \\ -15 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 3 \\ 2 \\ -4 \\ -1 \\ -2 \end{array}\right]\right\}.\]

Answer.

The dimension of \(W\) is \(3\).
