V8 - Dimension (ver. 763)

Explain how to find the dimension of

\[W=\mathrm{span}\ \left\{\left[\begin{array}{r} 0 \\ -1 \\ 1 \\ 1 \\ 2 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} -3 \\ -4 \\ -2 \\ -3 \\ -2 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 2 \\ -2 \\ 0 \\ 1 \\ 2 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 0 \\ 12 \\ 6 \\ 5 \\ 2 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} -12 \\ 21 \\ 9 \\ 2 \\ -4 \end{array}\right]\right\}.\]

Answer.

The dimension of \(W\) is \(3\).
