Explain how to find the dimension of

\[W=\mathrm{span}\ \left\{\left[\begin{array}{r} -3 \\ -1 \\ 0 \\ -3 \\ -3 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 1 \\ 1 \\ -4 \\ -4 \\ 0 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} -2 \\ 2 \\ -16 \\ -22 \\ -6 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 4 \\ -4 \\ 32 \\ 44 \\ 12 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 11 \\ -7 \\ 64 \\ 91 \\ 27 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} -4 \\ -1 \\ -4 \\ -4 \\ -2 \end{array}\right]\right\}.\]

Answer.

The dimension of \(W\) is \(3\).
