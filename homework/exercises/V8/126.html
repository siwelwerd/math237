V8 - Dimension (ver. 126)

Explain how to find the dimension of

\[W=\mathrm{span}\ \left\{\left[\begin{array}{r} 1 \\ -4 \\ -1 \\ -2 \\ -3 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 2 \\ 3 \\ 3 \\ -4 \\ 0 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} -1 \\ -2 \\ -4 \\ -3 \\ 3 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 1 \\ 2 \\ 0 \\ -1 \\ 2 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 1 \\ 19 \\ 10 \\ 4 \\ 8 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 0 \\ -7 \\ 2 \\ -3 \\ -9 \end{array}\right]\right\}.\]

Answer.

The dimension of \(W\) is \(4\).
