V8 - Dimension (ver. 941)

Explain how to find the dimension of

\[W=\mathrm{span}\ \left\{\left[\begin{array}{r} 2 \\ -3 \\ 3 \\ 0 \\ 1 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} -2 \\ 3 \\ 3 \\ -2 \\ -3 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} -8 \\ 12 \\ 6 \\ -6 \\ -10 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} -4 \\ 1 \\ -1 \\ -1 \\ 1 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 10 \\ -20 \\ -16 \\ 11 \\ 20 \end{array}\right]\right\}.\]

Answer.

The dimension of \(W\) is \(3\).
