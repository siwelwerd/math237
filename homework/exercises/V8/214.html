Explain how to find the dimension of

\[W=\mathrm{span}\ \left\{\left[\begin{array}{r} -3 \\ -4 \\ -4 \\ -3 \\ -4 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} -2 \\ 1 \\ 3 \\ 2 \\ -3 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 22 \\ 11 \\ 1 \\ 2 \\ 31 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 14 \\ 4 \\ -4 \\ -2 \\ 20 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} -31 \\ -1 \\ 21 \\ 13 \\ -45 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 23 \\ 5 \\ -9 \\ -5 \\ 33 \end{array}\right]\right\}.\]

Answer.

The dimension of \(W\) is \(2\).
