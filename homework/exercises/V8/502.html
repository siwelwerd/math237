Explain how to find the dimension of

\[W=\mathrm{span}\ \left\{\left[\begin{array}{r} -3 \\ -2 \\ -4 \\ -1 \\ 3 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} -4 \\ -1 \\ -1 \\ -4 \\ -2 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 7 \\ -2 \\ -8 \\ 13 \\ 17 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 23 \\ -3 \\ -17 \\ 37 \\ 43 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} -37 \\ 7 \\ 33 \\ -63 \\ -77 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} -39 \\ -1 \\ 13 \\ -53 \\ -51 \end{array}\right]\right\}.\]

Answer.

The dimension of \(W\) is \(2\).
