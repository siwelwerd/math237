Explain how to find the dimension of

\[W=\mathrm{span}\ \left\{\left[\begin{array}{r} 2 \\ -4 \\ -1 \\ -1 \\ 3 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 2 \\ 2 \\ -2 \\ 3 \\ -2 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 10 \\ -14 \\ -6 \\ -1 \\ 10 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 10 \\ -20 \\ -5 \\ -5 \\ 15 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 12 \\ -6 \\ -9 \\ 6 \\ 3 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 26 \\ -40 \\ -15 \\ -5 \\ 29 \end{array}\right]\right\}.\]

Answer.

The dimension of \(W\) is \(2\).
