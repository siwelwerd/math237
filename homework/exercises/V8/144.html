V8 - Dimension (ver. 144)

Explain how to find the dimension of

\[W=\mathrm{span}\ \left\{\left[\begin{array}{r} 3 \\ -2 \\ -2 \\ 0 \\ 0 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} -2 \\ -1 \\ -3 \\ -2 \\ 2 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 20 \\ -4 \\ 4 \\ 8 \\ -8 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 2 \\ -4 \\ -4 \\ -4 \\ -1 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} -50 \\ 0 \\ -24 \\ -32 \\ 22 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 35 \\ 0 \\ 20 \\ 20 \\ -20 \end{array}\right]\right\}.\]

Answer.

The dimension of \(W\) is \(3\).
