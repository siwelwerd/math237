V8 - Dimension (ver. 749)

Explain how to find the dimension of

\[W=\mathrm{span}\ \left\{\left[\begin{array}{r} -2 \\ 3 \\ 1 \\ -3 \\ 0 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 3 \\ 0 \\ -3 \\ 3 \\ -4 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} -5 \\ -6 \\ 7 \\ -3 \\ 12 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} -1 \\ -3 \\ -3 \\ -4 \\ -4 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 3 \\ -9 \\ -10 \\ -2 \\ -12 \end{array}\right]\right\}.\]

Answer.

The dimension of \(W\) is \(3\).
