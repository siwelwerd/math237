V8 - Dimension (ver. 330)

Explain how to find the dimension of

\[W=\mathrm{span}\ \left\{\left[\begin{array}{r} -4 \\ -2 \\ 1 \\ -2 \\ 2 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 2 \\ -1 \\ -3 \\ 3 \\ -1 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} -10 \\ 1 \\ 10 \\ -11 \\ 5 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 16 \\ -4 \\ -19 \\ 20 \\ -8 \end{array}\right]\right\}.\]

Answer.

The dimension of \(W\) is \(2\).
