Explain how to find the dimension of

\[W=\mathrm{span}\ \left\{\left[\begin{array}{r} -1 \\ 3 \\ -3 \\ -4 \\ -3 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} -1 \\ -2 \\ 2 \\ -4 \\ 3 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 2 \\ -3 \\ -2 \\ -2 \\ -3 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} -2 \\ -1 \\ -4 \\ -18 \\ -3 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 2 \\ 3 \\ -3 \\ 2 \\ 1 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 4 \\ -6 \\ -4 \\ -4 \\ -6 \end{array}\right]\right\}.\]

Answer.

The dimension of \(W\) is \(4\).
