Explain how to find the dimension of

\[W=\mathrm{span}\ \left\{\left[\begin{array}{r} 3 \\ 1 \\ 1 \\ -4 \\ -4 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} -2 \\ 3 \\ 0 \\ 0 \\ -1 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 2 \\ -1 \\ -4 \\ 1 \\ -4 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} -4 \\ 1 \\ 3 \\ -3 \\ -2 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 6 \\ -6 \\ 4 \\ -13 \\ -13 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} -4 \\ 1 \\ 3 \\ -4 \\ -2 \end{array}\right]\right\}.\]

Answer.

The dimension of \(W\) is \(5\).
