Explain how to find the dimension of

\[W=\mathrm{span}\ \left\{\left[\begin{array}{r} 0 \\ 2 \\ -1 \\ -1 \\ 1 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 0 \\ 0 \\ 1 \\ -2 \\ -1 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 0 \\ 8 \\ -1 \\ -10 \\ 1 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 0 \\ -4 \\ 4 \\ -2 \\ -4 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 0 \\ -26 \\ 9 \\ 21 \\ -9 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} -1 \\ 2 \\ -2 \\ -2 \\ -1 \end{array}\right]\right\}.\]

Answer.

The dimension of \(W\) is \(3\).
