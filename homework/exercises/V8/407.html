Explain how to find the dimension of

\[W=\mathrm{span}\ \left\{\left[\begin{array}{r} 3 \\ -1 \\ 3 \\ 0 \\ -3 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} -3 \\ -4 \\ 1 \\ -1 \\ 2 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} -3 \\ 21 \\ -19 \\ 4 \\ 7 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 6 \\ -47 \\ 42 \\ -9 \\ -15 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 2 \\ -3 \\ -2 \\ 0 \\ 0 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} -12 \\ 99 \\ -88 \\ 19 \\ 31 \end{array}\right]\right\}.\]

Answer.

The dimension of \(W\) is \(3\).
