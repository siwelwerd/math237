V8 - Dimension (ver. 517)

Explain how to find the dimension of

\[W=\mathrm{span}\ \left\{\left[\begin{array}{r} -1 \\ 0 \\ 0 \\ 0 \\ -4 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 1 \\ 0 \\ -2 \\ 2 \\ -4 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 0 \\ 0 \\ 6 \\ -6 \\ 24 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 0 \\ 2 \\ 3 \\ 1 \\ -2 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} -1 \\ 4 \\ 12 \\ -4 \\ 16 \end{array}\right]\right\}.\]

Answer.

The dimension of \(W\) is \(3\).
