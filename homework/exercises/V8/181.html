V8 - Dimension (ver. 181)

Explain how to find the dimension of

\[W=\mathrm{span}\ \left\{\left[\begin{array}{r} -3 \\ -2 \\ 1 \\ 1 \\ 2 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} -3 \\ 2 \\ 1 \\ -4 \\ -2 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 2 \\ -1 \\ -1 \\ 1 \\ 3 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} -13 \\ 4 \\ 5 \\ -9 \\ -8 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 36 \\ -7 \\ -13 \\ 23 \\ 13 \end{array}\right]\right\}.\]

Answer.

The dimension of \(W\) is \(3\).
