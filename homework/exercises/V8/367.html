Explain how to find the dimension of

\[W=\mathrm{span}\ \left\{\left[\begin{array}{r} 0 \\ 2 \\ 3 \\ 3 \\ 0 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 3 \\ -2 \\ -1 \\ -1 \\ -3 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 3 \\ -2 \\ -1 \\ 3 \\ 3 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} -2 \\ 3 \\ 3 \\ 1 \\ 3 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 3 \\ -9 \\ -11 \\ -13 \\ -18 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 14 \\ -15 \\ -13 \\ -3 \\ -3 \end{array}\right]\right\}.\]

Answer.

The dimension of \(W\) is \(4\).
