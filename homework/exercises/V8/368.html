V8 - Dimension (ver. 368)

Explain how to find the dimension of

\[W=\mathrm{span}\ \left\{\left[\begin{array}{r} -1 \\ 3 \\ 3 \\ 1 \\ -2 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 3 \\ -2 \\ 0 \\ -1 \\ 0 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 11 \\ -5 \\ 3 \\ -3 \\ -2 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 20 \\ -4 \\ 12 \\ -4 \\ -8 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} -2 \\ 0 \\ -3 \\ -4 \\ 2 \end{array}\right]\right\}.\]

Answer.

The dimension of \(W\) is \(3\).
