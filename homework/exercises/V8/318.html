V8 - Dimension (ver. 318)

Explain how to find the dimension of

\[W=\mathrm{span}\ \left\{\left[\begin{array}{r} 3 \\ 2 \\ -3 \\ 0 \\ 3 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 1 \\ 3 \\ -1 \\ 2 \\ -4 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} -3 \\ 5 \\ 3 \\ 6 \\ -18 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} -11 \\ 2 \\ 11 \\ 8 \\ -31 \end{array}\right]\right\}.\]

Answer.

The dimension of \(W\) is \(2\).
