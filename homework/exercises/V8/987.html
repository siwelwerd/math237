Explain how to find the dimension of

\[W=\mathrm{span}\ \left\{\left[\begin{array}{r} 1 \\ 3 \\ -2 \\ 3 \\ -2 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 2 \\ -4 \\ 1 \\ 2 \\ -4 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 0 \\ -20 \\ 10 \\ -8 \\ 0 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 4 \\ 42 \\ -23 \\ 24 \\ -8 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 1 \\ -4 \\ 1 \\ 2 \\ 2 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 0 \\ 3 \\ -3 \\ -2 \\ -1 \end{array}\right]\right\}.\]

Answer.

The dimension of \(W\) is \(4\).
