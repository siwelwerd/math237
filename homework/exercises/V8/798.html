Explain how to find the dimension of

\[W=\mathrm{span}\ \left\{\left[\begin{array}{r} -3 \\ -2 \\ 3 \\ -1 \\ 1 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 0 \\ -1 \\ 2 \\ 3 \\ -3 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 15 \\ 14 \\ -23 \\ -7 \\ 7 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} -36 \\ -37 \\ 62 \\ 27 \\ -27 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 81 \\ 79 \\ -131 \\ -48 \\ 48 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 1 \\ -3 \\ 0 \\ -3 \\ 2 \end{array}\right]\right\}.\]

Answer.

The dimension of \(W\) is \(3\).
