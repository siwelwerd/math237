V8 - Dimension (ver. 186)

Explain how to find the dimension of

\[W=\mathrm{span}\ \left\{\left[\begin{array}{r} 3 \\ 2 \\ -1 \\ 0 \\ 1 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 3 \\ -1 \\ -2 \\ -1 \\ -4 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} -9 \\ 6 \\ 7 \\ 4 \\ 17 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 15 \\ -8 \\ -11 \\ -6 \\ -25 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} -3 \\ 10 \\ 5 \\ 4 \\ 19 \end{array}\right]\right\}.\]

Answer.

The dimension of \(W\) is \(2\).
