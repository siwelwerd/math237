Explain how to find the dimension of

\[W=\mathrm{span}\ \left\{\left[\begin{array}{r} -4 \\ 3 \\ 3 \\ -2 \\ -4 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 2 \\ 3 \\ 1 \\ 2 \\ 2 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 24 \\ -9 \\ -13 \\ 14 \\ 24 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} -30 \\ 0 \\ 10 \\ -20 \\ -30 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} -3 \\ 3 \\ -4 \\ 1 \\ 1 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} -12 \\ -18 \\ -6 \\ -12 \\ -12 \end{array}\right]\right\}.\]

Answer.

The dimension of \(W\) is \(3\).
