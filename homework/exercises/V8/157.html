V8 - Dimension (ver. 157)

Explain how to find the dimension of

\[W=\mathrm{span}\ \left\{\left[\begin{array}{r} -2 \\ 2 \\ -1 \\ -1 \\ -3 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 1 \\ -1 \\ 3 \\ -2 \\ 1 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} -2 \\ 2 \\ -1 \\ -1 \\ -3 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 3 \\ -3 \\ -6 \\ 9 \\ 6 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} -2 \\ 2 \\ 9 \\ -11 \\ -5 \end{array}\right]\right\}.\]

Answer.

The dimension of \(W\) is \(2\).
