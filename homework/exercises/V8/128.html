V8 - Dimension (ver. 128)

Explain how to find the dimension of

\[W=\mathrm{span}\ \left\{\left[\begin{array}{r} 3 \\ 3 \\ 3 \\ 3 \\ -3 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} -3 \\ -1 \\ -2 \\ 3 \\ -4 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 0 \\ 3 \\ -1 \\ 3 \\ 0 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} -12 \\ -17 \\ -7 \\ -9 \\ -2 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 12 \\ 14 \\ 3 \\ -6 \\ 23 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 12 \\ 8 \\ 10 \\ 0 \\ 2 \end{array}\right]\right\}.\]

Answer.

The dimension of \(W\) is \(3\).
