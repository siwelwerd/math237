Explain how to find the dimension of

\[W=\mathrm{span}\ \left\{\left[\begin{array}{r} 3 \\ -4 \\ 1 \\ 2 \\ 3 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 3 \\ 0 \\ 3 \\ 3 \\ -1 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 6 \\ 4 \\ 8 \\ 7 \\ -6 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} -18 \\ -20 \\ -28 \\ -23 \\ 26 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} -9 \\ -16 \\ -17 \\ -13 \\ 19 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 0 \\ -3 \\ 0 \\ -4 \\ -1 \end{array}\right]\right\}.\]

Answer.

The dimension of \(W\) is \(3\).
