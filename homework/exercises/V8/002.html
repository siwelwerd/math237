V8 - Dimension (ver. 2)

Explain how to find the dimension of

\[W=\mathrm{span}\ \left\{\left[\begin{array}{r} -1 \\ -4 \\ -1 \\ -2 \\ 1 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 3 \\ 2 \\ -2 \\ 1 \\ -1 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 5 \\ 10 \\ 0 \\ 5 \\ -3 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} -4 \\ 1 \\ 2 \\ -2 \\ 2 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} -9 \\ -20 \\ 4 \\ -5 \\ 3 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 0 \\ 17 \\ 6 \\ 6 \\ -2 \end{array}\right]\right\}.\]

Answer.

The dimension of \(W\) is \(3\).
