V8 - Dimension (ver. 398)

Explain how to find the dimension of

\[W=\mathrm{span}\ \left\{\left[\begin{array}{r} -2 \\ -3 \\ -4 \\ -4 \\ -1 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} -1 \\ 2 \\ -3 \\ -4 \\ 2 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 11 \\ 6 \\ 25 \\ 28 \\ -2 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} -25 \\ -13 \\ -57 \\ -64 \\ 5 \end{array}\right]\right\}.\]

Answer.

The dimension of \(W\) is \(2\).
