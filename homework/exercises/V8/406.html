Explain how to find the dimension of

\[W=\mathrm{span}\ \left\{\left[\begin{array}{r} 3 \\ -3 \\ -3 \\ -1 \\ 1 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 0 \\ 0 \\ 2 \\ 0 \\ -2 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 1 \\ -4 \\ -2 \\ -1 \\ 1 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 2 \\ -8 \\ -4 \\ -2 \\ 2 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} -6 \\ 24 \\ 10 \\ 6 \\ -4 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} -7 \\ 1 \\ 1 \\ 1 \\ 3 \end{array}\right]\right\}.\]

Answer.

The dimension of \(W\) is \(3\).
