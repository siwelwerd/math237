V8 - Dimension (ver. 607)

Explain how to find the dimension of

\[W=\mathrm{span}\ \left\{\left[\begin{array}{r} -1 \\ 2 \\ 2 \\ 1 \\ 3 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 2 \\ -1 \\ 3 \\ -4 \\ 3 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} -8 \\ 4 \\ -12 \\ 16 \\ -12 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 14 \\ -4 \\ 28 \\ -30 \\ 30 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 0 \\ 2 \\ -1 \\ 2 \\ 1 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} -29 \\ 10 \\ -54 \\ 61 \\ -57 \end{array}\right]\right\}.\]

Answer.

The dimension of \(W\) is \(3\).
