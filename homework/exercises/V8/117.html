V8 - Dimension (ver. 117)

Explain how to find the dimension of

\[W=\mathrm{span}\ \left\{\left[\begin{array}{r} -1 \\ -4 \\ -1 \\ -2 \\ 1 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 1 \\ -3 \\ -3 \\ -3 \\ 2 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 0 \\ -14 \\ -8 \\ -10 \\ 6 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 5 \\ 6 \\ -3 \\ 0 \\ 1 \end{array}\right]\right\}.\]

Answer.

The dimension of \(W\) is \(2\).
