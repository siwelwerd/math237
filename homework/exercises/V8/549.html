Explain how to find the dimension of

\[W=\mathrm{span}\ \left\{\left[\begin{array}{r} 3 \\ 3 \\ -4 \\ 3 \\ 1 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} -4 \\ 2 \\ -1 \\ -4 \\ -1 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} -7 \\ -1 \\ 3 \\ -7 \\ -2 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} -3 \\ 0 \\ 0 \\ 3 \\ -2 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} -6 \\ -12 \\ 16 \\ -18 \\ 0 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 22 \\ -5 \\ 0 \\ 16 \\ 7 \end{array}\right]\right\}.\]

Answer.

The dimension of \(W\) is \(3\).
