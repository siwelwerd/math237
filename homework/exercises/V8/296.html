V8 - Dimension (ver. 296)

Explain how to find the dimension of

\[W=\mathrm{span}\ \left\{\left[\begin{array}{r} 3 \\ 1 \\ -4 \\ -4 \\ -4 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} -4 \\ 0 \\ -3 \\ -2 \\ -1 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} -4 \\ -1 \\ 1 \\ -1 \\ 1 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 0 \\ -1 \\ 0 \\ 2 \\ 2 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} -9 \\ 2 \\ -5 \\ -14 \\ -9 \end{array}\right]\right\}.\]

Answer.

The dimension of \(W\) is \(4\).
