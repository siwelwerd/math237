V8 - Dimension (ver. 174)

Explain how to find the dimension of

\[W=\mathrm{span}\ \left\{\left[\begin{array}{r} 2 \\ 3 \\ 3 \\ -2 \\ 0 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} -1 \\ -2 \\ 0 \\ -1 \\ 1 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 0 \\ -4 \\ 1 \\ -4 \\ 1 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} -2 \\ -11 \\ -1 \\ -6 \\ 2 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} -4 \\ -9 \\ -8 \\ 4 \\ -1 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 3 \\ 2 \\ 3 \\ 0 \\ 0 \end{array}\right]\right\}.\]

Answer.

The dimension of \(W\) is \(4\).
