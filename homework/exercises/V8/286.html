V8 - Dimension (ver. 286)

Explain how to find the dimension of

\[W=\mathrm{span}\ \left\{\left[\begin{array}{r} -4 \\ -3 \\ 2 \\ 0 \\ -1 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} -4 \\ 2 \\ 3 \\ -3 \\ 0 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 28 \\ 1 \\ -18 \\ 12 \\ 3 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 36 \\ 7 \\ -22 \\ 12 \\ 5 \end{array}\right]\right\}.\]

Answer.

The dimension of \(W\) is \(2\).
