Explain how to find the dimension of

\[W=\mathrm{span}\ \left\{\left[\begin{array}{r} -1 \\ -1 \\ -3 \\ -2 \\ 3 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} -3 \\ 1 \\ -1 \\ 1 \\ -1 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 4 \\ 0 \\ 4 \\ 1 \\ -2 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} -8 \\ -4 \\ -16 \\ -9 \\ 14 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} -14 \\ 2 \\ -10 \\ 0 \\ 2 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} -7 \\ 5 \\ 3 \\ 7 \\ -9 \end{array}\right]\right\}.\]

Answer.

The dimension of \(W\) is \(2\).
