Explain how to find the dimension of

\[W=\mathrm{span}\ \left\{\left[\begin{array}{r} -4 \\ -4 \\ -2 \\ -4 \\ 1 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} -4 \\ 1 \\ -3 \\ 1 \\ 2 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} -1 \\ -4 \\ 3 \\ -4 \\ -3 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 4 \\ 14 \\ 0 \\ 14 \\ 1 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} -9 \\ -47 \\ 6 \\ -47 \\ -8 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} -9 \\ -37 \\ 4 \\ -37 \\ -6 \end{array}\right]\right\}.\]

Answer.

The dimension of \(W\) is \(3\).
