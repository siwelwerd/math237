V8 - Dimension (ver. 705)

Explain how to find the dimension of

\[W=\mathrm{span}\ \left\{\left[\begin{array}{r} 0 \\ -1 \\ 1 \\ 1 \\ -1 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 1 \\ -4 \\ 1 \\ -3 \\ -4 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} -3 \\ 10 \\ -1 \\ 11 \\ 10 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 1 \\ -3 \\ 0 \\ 0 \\ 1 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 8 \\ -29 \\ 5 \\ -31 \\ -33 \end{array}\right]\right\}.\]

Answer.

The dimension of \(W\) is \(3\).
