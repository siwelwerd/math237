V8 - Dimension (ver. 99)

Explain how to find the dimension of

\[W=\mathrm{span}\ \left\{\left[\begin{array}{r} -2 \\ -1 \\ 2 \\ 3 \\ -1 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 2 \\ -1 \\ -2 \\ -4 \\ -1 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} -6 \\ -1 \\ 6 \\ 10 \\ -1 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 1 \\ 2 \\ -2 \\ 3 \\ 0 \end{array}\right]\right\}.\]

Answer.

The dimension of \(W\) is \(3\).
