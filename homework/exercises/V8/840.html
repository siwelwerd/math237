Explain how to find the dimension of

\[W=\mathrm{span}\ \left\{\left[\begin{array}{r} -3 \\ -2 \\ -3 \\ 3 \\ 3 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} -1 \\ -1 \\ 3 \\ -1 \\ -3 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} -5 \\ -2 \\ -21 \\ 13 \\ 21 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} -12 \\ -5 \\ -48 \\ 30 \\ 48 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} -1 \\ -4 \\ 1 \\ 2 \\ -2 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 0 \\ 2 \\ -3 \\ 2 \\ -2 \end{array}\right]\right\}.\]

Answer.

The dimension of \(W\) is \(4\).
