Explain how to find the dimension of

\[W=\mathrm{span}\ \left\{\left[\begin{array}{r} -2 \\ -3 \\ 1 \\ -3 \\ -1 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 2 \\ 2 \\ 3 \\ -4 \\ 3 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 6 \\ 10 \\ -7 \\ 16 \\ 1 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} -4 \\ -2 \\ -1 \\ 3 \\ 0 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} -8 \\ -21 \\ 27 \\ -56 \\ 4 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 10 \\ 5 \\ 9 \\ -17 \\ 5 \end{array}\right]\right\}.\]

Answer.

The dimension of \(W\) is \(3\).
