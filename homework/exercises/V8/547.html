V8 - Dimension (ver. 547)

Explain how to find the dimension of

\[W=\mathrm{span}\ \left\{\left[\begin{array}{r} 3 \\ 1 \\ 1 \\ -1 \\ -2 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 0 \\ -1 \\ -3 \\ 2 \\ -4 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 0 \\ -4 \\ -12 \\ 8 \\ -16 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 3 \\ 11 \\ 31 \\ -21 \\ 38 \end{array}\right]\right\}.\]

Answer.

The dimension of \(W\) is \(2\).
