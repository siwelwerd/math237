Explain how to find the dimension of

\[W=\mathrm{span}\ \left\{\left[\begin{array}{r} 0 \\ -3 \\ -4 \\ 2 \\ -4 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 0 \\ -3 \\ -2 \\ 1 \\ 0 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 0 \\ -18 \\ -20 \\ 10 \\ -16 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} -3 \\ 3 \\ -3 \\ -1 \\ -4 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 0 \\ -15 \\ -16 \\ 8 \\ -12 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} -3 \\ -3 \\ 3 \\ -4 \\ 3 \end{array}\right]\right\}.\]

Answer.

The dimension of \(W\) is \(4\).
