Answer.

The vectors \(\left[\begin{array}{r} -1 \\ 4 \\ -3 \\ -4 \\ 2 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 1 \\ 4 \\ 1 \\ -4 \\ -5 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} -3 \\ 4 \\ -7 \\ -4 \\ 9 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} -11 \\ 4 \\ -23 \\ -4 \\ 37 \end{array}\right] , \text{ and } \left[\begin{array}{r} -14 \\ 0 \\ -28 \\ 0 \\ 49 \end{array}\right]\) are not a basis of \(\mathbb{R}^5\).
