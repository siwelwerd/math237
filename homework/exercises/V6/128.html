The vectors \(\left[\begin{array}{r} 4 \\ 4 \\ 4 \\ 3 \\ -3 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} -3 \\ -1 \\ -2 \\ 4 \\ -4 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 17 \\ 11 \\ 14 \\ -6 \\ 6 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} -49 \\ -35 \\ -42 \\ 7 \\ -7 \end{array}\right] , \text{ and } \left[\begin{array}{r} 135 \\ 93 \\ 114 \\ -30 \\ 30 \end{array}\right]\) are not a basis of \(\mathbb{R}^5\).
