Find a basis for the subspace of \(M_{2,2}\)

\[W=\mathrm{span}\ \left\{\left[\begin{array}{cc} -6 & -5 \\ -3 & 2 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{cc} 0 & -1 \\ -6 & 3 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{cc} 15 & 16 \\ 3 & 0 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{cc} -5 & 4 \\ -6 & 3 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{cc} 3 & -3 \\ -6 & 0 \end{array}\right]\right\}.\]

Be sure to explain why your result is a basis.

Answer.

A basis of \(W\) is \(\left\{\left[\begin{array}{cc} -6 & -5 \\ -3 & 2 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{cc} 0 & -1 \\ -6 & 3 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{cc} 15 & 16 \\ 3 & 0 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{cc} -5 & 4 \\ -6 & 3 \end{array}\right]\right\}\).
