Find a basis for the subspace of \(M_{2,2}\)

\[W=\mathrm{span}\ \left\{\left[\begin{array}{cc} 10 & 2 \\ 10 & -12 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{cc} -6 & 10 \\ -2 & 14 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{cc} -2 & -6 \\ -4 & -1 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{cc} 4 & 12 \\ 8 & 2 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{cc} 5 & 1 \\ 5 & -6 \end{array}\right]\right\}.\]

Be sure to explain why your result is a basis.

Answer.

A basis of \(W\) is \(\left\{\left[\begin{array}{cc} 10 & 2 \\ 10 & -12 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{cc} -6 & 10 \\ -2 & 14 \end{array}\right]\right\}\).
