\[W=\mathrm{span}\ \left\{2 \, x^{3} - x + 3 , -5 \, x^{3} - 2 \, x + 21 , 16 \, x^{3} - 2 \, x - 14 , -6 \, x^{3} + 10 , 3 \, x^{3} - 5\right\}.\]
 Be sure to explain why your result is a basis.


  \end{exerciseStatement}

   A basis of \(W\) is  \(\left\{2 \, x^{3} - x + 3 , -5 \, x^{3} - 2 \, x + 21\right\}\).
