Find a basis for the subspace of \(M_{2,2}\)

\[W=\mathrm{span}\ \left\{\left[\begin{array}{cc} -6 & 5 \\ 0 & -1 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{cc} 7 & -6 \\ 3 & 7 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{cc} -3 & 4 \\ 0 & -1 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{cc} 0 & -5 \\ -3 & -5 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{cc} 5 & -4 \\ -3 & -5 \end{array}\right]\right\}.\]

Be sure to explain why your result is a basis.

Answer.

A basis of \(W\) is \(\left\{\left[\begin{array}{cc} -6 & 5 \\ 0 & -1 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{cc} 7 & -6 \\ 3 & 7 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{cc} -3 & 4 \\ 0 & -1 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{cc} 0 & -5 \\ -3 & -5 \end{array}\right]\right\}\).
