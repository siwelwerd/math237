Find a basis for the subspace of \(M_{2,2}\)

\[W=\mathrm{span}\ \left\{\left[\begin{array}{cc} -5 & -2 \\ 3 & 0 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{cc} 40 & -12 \\ -17 & 28 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{cc} 10 & -8 \\ -3 & 12 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{cc} -30 & 16 \\ 11 & -28 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{cc} -5 & 2 \\ 2 & -4 \end{array}\right]\right\}.\]

Be sure to explain why your result is a basis.

Answer.

A basis of \(W\) is \(\left\{\left[\begin{array}{cc} -5 & -2 \\ 3 & 0 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{cc} 40 & -12 \\ -17 & 28 \end{array}\right]\right\}\).
