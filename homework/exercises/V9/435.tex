 Be sure to explain why your result is a basis.


  \end{exerciseStatement}

   A basis of \(W\) is  \(\left\{-3 \, x^{3} + x^{2} - 3 , -13 \, x^{3} - 9 \, x^{2} + 15 \, x - 13 , 2 \, x^{3} + 5 \, x - 1\right\}\).
