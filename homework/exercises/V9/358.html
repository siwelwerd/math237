Find a basis for the subspace of \(M_{2,2}\)

\[W=\mathrm{span}\ \left\{\left[\begin{array}{cc} -26 & 4 \\ 3 & -7 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{cc} 15 & -18 \\ 12 & 23 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{cc} -2 & 4 \\ -3 & -5 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{cc} 11 & -10 \\ 6 & 13 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{cc} -5 & -2 \\ 3 & 2 \end{array}\right]\right\}.\]

Be sure to explain why your result is a basis.

Answer.

A basis of \(W\) is \(\left\{\left[\begin{array}{cc} -26 & 4 \\ 3 & -7 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{cc} 15 & -18 \\ 12 & 23 \end{array}\right]\right\}\).
