\[W=\mathrm{span}\ \left\{-6 \, x^{3} - 6 \, x^{2} + 5 \, x - 6 , x^{3} - 5 \, x^{2} - x - 5 , -2 \, x^{3} - 62 \, x^{2} - 62 , 3 \, x^{3} + 21 \, x^{2} - 2 \, x + 21 , -x^{3} - x^{2} - 2\right\}.\]

Answer.

A basis of \(W\) is \(\left\{-6 \, x^{3} - 6 \, x^{2} + 5 \, x - 6 , x^{3} - 5 \, x^{2} - x - 5 , -x^{3} - x^{2} - 2\right\}\).
