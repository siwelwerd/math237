Find a basis for the subspace of \(M_{2,2}\)

\[W=\mathrm{span}\ \left\{\left[\begin{array}{cc} 5 & -3 \\ -1 & 3 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{cc} -3 & 0 \\ -2 & 0 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{cc} -6 & -1 \\ -2 & 1 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{cc} -27 & -5 \\ -8 & 5 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{cc} -12 & -2 \\ -4 & 2 \end{array}\right]\right\}.\]

Be sure to explain why your result is a basis.

Answer.

A basis of \(W\) is \(\left\{\left[\begin{array}{cc} 5 & -3 \\ -1 & 3 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{cc} -3 & 0 \\ -2 & 0 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{cc} -6 & -1 \\ -2 & 1 \end{array}\right]\right\}\).
