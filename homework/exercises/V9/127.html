Find a basis for the subspace of \(M_{2,2}\)

\[W=\mathrm{span}\ \left\{\left[\begin{array}{cc} 1 & 18 \\ -18 & 3 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{cc} 2 & 1 \\ -3 & 3 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{cc} -1 & -1 \\ 3 & -6 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{cc} -1 & 3 \\ 4 & 0 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{cc} 1 & -5 \\ 3 & 4 \end{array}\right]\right\}.\]

Be sure to explain why your result is a basis.

Answer.

A basis of \(W\) is \(\left\{\left[\begin{array}{cc} 1 & 18 \\ -18 & 3 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{cc} 2 & 1 \\ -3 & 3 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{cc} -1 & -1 \\ 3 & -6 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{cc} -1 & 3 \\ 4 & 0 \end{array}\right]\right\}\).
