\[W=\mathrm{span}\ \left\{\left[\begin{array}{cc} 28 & -28 \\ -42 & -21 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{cc} 16 & -12 \\ -62 & -33 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{cc} -3 & 4 \\ -5 & -3 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{cc} -5 & 4 \\ 17 & 9 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{cc} 4 & -4 \\ -6 & -3 \end{array}\right]\right\}.\]

A basis of \(W\) is \(\left\{\left[\begin{array}{cc} 28 & -28 \\ -42 & -21 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{cc} 16 & -12 \\ -62 & -33 \end{array}\right]\right\}\).
