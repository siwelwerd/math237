Find a basis for the subspace of \(M_{2,2}\)

\[W=\mathrm{span}\ \left\{\left[\begin{array}{cc} -4 & -6 \\ -1 & 3 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{cc} -6 & -3 \\ -5 & 4 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{cc} 4 & -2 \\ -6 & -3 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{cc} -6 & -15 \\ -33 & 3 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{cc} 6 & 15 \\ 33 & -3 \end{array}\right]\right\}.\]

Be sure to explain why your result is a basis.

Answer.

A basis of \(W\) is \(\left\{\left[\begin{array}{cc} -4 & -6 \\ -1 & 3 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{cc} -6 & -3 \\ -5 & 4 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{cc} 4 & -2 \\ -6 & -3 \end{array}\right]\right\}\).
