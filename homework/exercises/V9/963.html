\[W=\mathrm{span}\ \left\{-7 \, x^{3} + 62 \, x^{2} + 12 \, x - 29 , 3 \, x^{3} + 30 \, x^{2} - 12 , -5 \, x^{3} - 6 \, x^{2} + 4 \, x + 1 , 4 \, x^{3} - 4 \, x^{2} - 4 \, x + 3 , -5 \, x^{3} - 50 \, x^{2} + 20\right\}.\]
