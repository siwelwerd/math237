Find a basis for the subspace of \(M_{2,2}\)

\[W=\mathrm{span}\ \left\{\left[\begin{array}{cc} -5 & -35 \\ -30 & -25 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{cc} 9 & -51 \\ -24 & -33 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{cc} 2 & 14 \\ 12 & 10 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{cc} -2 & 5 \\ 1 & 3 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{cc} -3 & -2 \\ -5 & -2 \end{array}\right]\right\}.\]

Be sure to explain why your result is a basis.

Answer.

A basis of \(W\) is \(\left\{\left[\begin{array}{cc} -5 & -35 \\ -30 & -25 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{cc} 9 & -51 \\ -24 & -33 \end{array}\right]\right\}\).
