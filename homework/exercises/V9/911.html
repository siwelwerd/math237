Find a basis for the subspace of \(M_{2,2}\)

\[W=\mathrm{span}\ \left\{\left[\begin{array}{cc} -5 & 5 \\ -2 & 2 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{cc} 6 & -27 \\ -3 & -24 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{cc} 3 & 0 \\ -1 & 2 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{cc} -4 & -6 \\ 2 & -3 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{cc} 0 & -6 \\ -6 & -6 \end{array}\right]\right\}.\]

Be sure to explain why your result is a basis.

Answer.

A basis of \(W\) is \(\left\{\left[\begin{array}{cc} -5 & 5 \\ -2 & 2 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{cc} 6 & -27 \\ -3 & -24 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{cc} 3 & 0 \\ -1 & 2 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{cc} -4 & -6 \\ 2 & -3 \end{array}\right]\right\}\).
