Find a basis for the subspace of \(M_{2,2}\)

\[W=\mathrm{span}\ \left\{\left[\begin{array}{cc} 2 & -4 \\ 1 & 1 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{cc} -4 & -4 \\ -4 & 4 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{cc} -13 & -14 \\ -10 & 11 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{cc} -5 & 3 \\ 3 & 1 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{cc} -5 & -6 \\ -2 & 3 \end{array}\right]\right\}.\]

Be sure to explain why your result is a basis.

Answer.

A basis of \(W\) is \(\left\{\left[\begin{array}{cc} 2 & -4 \\ 1 & 1 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{cc} -4 & -4 \\ -4 & 4 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{cc} -13 & -14 \\ -10 & 11 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{cc} -5 & 3 \\ 3 & 1 \end{array}\right]\right\}\).
