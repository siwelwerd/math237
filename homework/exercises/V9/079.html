Find a basis for the subspace of \(M_{2,2}\)

\[W=\mathrm{span}\ \left\{\left[\begin{array}{cc} 5 & -15 \\ 5 & 2 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{cc} 0 & 3 \\ -5 & -5 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{cc} 1 & -6 \\ -4 & -6 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{cc} -3 & -3 \\ -3 & -4 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{cc} 4 & -3 \\ -1 & -2 \end{array}\right]\right\}.\]

Be sure to explain why your result is a basis.

Answer.

A basis of \(W\) is \(\left\{\left[\begin{array}{cc} 5 & -15 \\ 5 & 2 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{cc} 0 & 3 \\ -5 & -5 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{cc} 1 & -6 \\ -4 & -6 \end{array}\right]\right\}\).
