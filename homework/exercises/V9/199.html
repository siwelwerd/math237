Find a basis for the subspace of \(M_{2,2}\)

\[W=\mathrm{span}\ \left\{\left[\begin{array}{cc} 0 & 0 \\ 0 & 0 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{cc} -1 & -1 \\ 2 & -4 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{cc} -1 & 2 \\ 5 & 4 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{cc} -4 & 1 \\ 8 & -11 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{cc} 2 & -3 \\ -4 & 3 \end{array}\right]\right\}.\]

Be sure to explain why your result is a basis.

Answer.

A basis of \(W\) is \(\left\{\left[\begin{array}{cc} -1 & -1 \\ 2 & -4 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{cc} -1 & 2 \\ 5 & 4 \end{array}\right] \left[\begin{array}{cc} -4 & 1 \\ 8 & -11 \end{array}\right]\right\}\).
