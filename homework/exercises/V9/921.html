Find a basis for the subspace of \(M_{2,2}\)

\[W=\mathrm{span}\ \left\{\left[\begin{array}{cc} -6 & 0 \\ -1 & 1 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{cc} 5 & 2 \\ -5 & 4 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{cc} -1 & -4 \\ -4 & -5 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{cc} -10 & -4 \\ 7 & -17 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{cc} 2 & -1 \\ -6 & -5 \end{array}\right]\right\}.\]

Be sure to explain why your result is a basis.

Answer.

A basis of \(W\) is \(\left\{\left[\begin{array}{cc} -6 & 0 \\ -1 & 1 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{cc} 5 & 2 \\ -5 & 4 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{cc} -1 & -4 \\ -4 & -5 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{cc} -10 & -4 \\ 7 & -17 \end{array}\right]\right\}\).
