\[W=\mathrm{span}\ \left\{9 \, x^{3} - 9 \, x^{2} - 12 \, x + 12 , -3 \, x^{3} + 3 \, x^{2} + 4 \, x - 4 , -5 \, x^{3} - 3 \, x^{2} + x - 1 , -x^{3} + 2 \, x^{2} - 4 \, x + 3 , -16 \, x^{3} + 14 \, x^{2} + 32 \, x - 30\right\}.\]

A basis of \(W\) is \(\left\{9 \, x^{3} - 9 \, x^{2} - 12 \, x + 12 , -5 \, x^{3} - 3 \, x^{2} + x - 1 -x^{3} + 2 \, x^{2} - 4 \, x + 3\right\}\).
