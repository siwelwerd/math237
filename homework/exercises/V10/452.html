V10 - Basis of Solution Space (ver. 452)

Find a basis for the solution space of the homogeneous system 10 x_ 1 + 22 x_ 2 -4 x_ 3 -6 x_ 4 &= 0 -7 x_ 1 -13 x_ 2 + 4 x_ 3 + 3 x_ 4 &= 0 1 x_ 1 -7 x_ 2 -5 x_ 3 + 4 x_ 4 &= 0 10 x_ 1 + 22 x_ 2 -4 x_ 3 -6 x_ 4 &= 0

Answer.

A basis is

\[\left\{\left[\begin{array}{c} \frac{3}{2} \\ -\frac{1}{2} \\ 1 \\ 0 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{c} -\frac{1}{2} \\ \frac{1}{2} \\ 0 \\ 1 \end{array}\right]\right\}.\]
