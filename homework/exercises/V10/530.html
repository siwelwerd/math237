V10 - Basis of Solution Space (ver. 530)

Find a basis for the solution space of the homogeneous system 5 x_ 1 + 2 x_ 2 -5 x_ 3 -15 x_ 4 &= 0 -6 x_ 1 -5 x_ 2 -6 x_ 3 + 6 x_ 4 &= 0 5 x_ 1 + 5 x_ 2 + 2 x_ 3 -8 x_ 4 &= 0 -1 x_ 1 + 3 x_ 2 -1 x_ 3 + 1 x_ 4 &= 0

Answer.

A basis is

\[\left\{\left[\begin{array}{c} 2 \\ 0 \\ -1 \\ 1 \end{array}\right]\right\}.\]
