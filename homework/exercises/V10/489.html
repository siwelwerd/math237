V10 - Basis of Solution Space (ver. 489)

Find a basis for the solution space of the homogeneous system 11 x_ 1 + 5 x_ 2 -1 x_ 3 + 4 x_ 4 -4 x_ 5 &= 0 -4 x_ 1 + 4 x_ 2 -2 x_ 3 -3 x_ 4 + 5 x_ 5 &= 0 -21 x_ 1 -4 x_ 2 + 5 x_ 3 -3 x_ 4 + 3 x_ 5 &= 0 -5 x_ 1 + 2 x_ 2 + 3 x_ 3 -3 x_ 4 -2 x_ 5 &= 0

Answer.

A basis is

\[\left\{\left[\begin{array}{c} \frac{1}{2} \\ 0 \\ \frac{3}{2} \\ 0 \\ 1 \end{array}\right]\right\}.\]
