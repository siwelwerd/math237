V10 - Basis of Solution Space (ver. 616)

Find a basis for the solution space of the homogeneous system 1 x_ 1 + 1 x_ 4 &= 0 2 x_ 1 -2 x_ 2 -3 x_ 3 + 8 x_ 4 &= 0 3 x_ 1 -3 x_ 3 + 9 x_ 4 &= 0 4 x_ 1 -3 x_ 2 -6 x_ 3 + 16 x_ 4 &= 0

Answer.

A basis is

\[\left\{\left[\begin{array}{c} -1 \\ 0 \\ 2 \\ 1 \end{array}\right]\right\}.\]
