Answer.

A basis is

\[\left\{\left[\begin{array}{c} 0 \\ 0 \\ -2 \\ 1 \\ 0 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{c} 0 \\ -\frac{1}{2} \\ -\frac{1}{2} \\ 0 \\ 1 \end{array}\right]\right\}.\]
