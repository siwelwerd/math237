V10 - Basis of Solution Space (ver. 467)

Find a basis for the solution space of the homogeneous system -1 x_ 1 -7 x_ 2 + 5 x_ 3 -3 x_ 4 &= 0 -3 x_ 1 -6 x_ 2 + 6 x_ 4 &= 0 5 x_ 1 + 16 x_ 2 -6 x_ 3 -4 x_ 4 &= 0 5 x_ 1 + 13 x_ 2 -3 x_ 3 -7 x_ 4 &= 0

Answer.

A basis is

\[\left\{\left[\begin{array}{c} -2 \\ 1 \\ 1 \\ 0 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{c} 4 \\ -1 \\ 0 \\ 1 \end{array}\right]\right\}.\]
