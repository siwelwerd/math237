V10 - Basis of Solution Space (ver. 222)

Find a basis for the solution space of the homogeneous system -3 x_ 1 + 1 x_ 2 &= 0 -12 x_ 1 + 4 x_ 2 + 2 x_ 3 &= 0 -15 x_ 1 + 5 x_ 2 + 1 x_ 3 &= 0 -15 x_ 1 + 5 x_ 2 -3 x_ 3 &= 0

Answer.

A basis is

\[\left\{\left[\begin{array}{c} \frac{1}{3} \\ 1 \\ 0 \end{array}\right]\right\}.\]
