V10 - Basis of Solution Space (ver. 23)

Find a basis for the solution space of the homogeneous system -5 x_ 1 + 20 x_ 2 -5 x_ 3 &= 0 -5 x_ 1 + 10 x_ 2 &= 0 -2 x_ 1 + 16 x_ 2 -6 x_ 3 &= 0 4 x_ 1 -10 x_ 2 + 1 x_ 3 &= 0

Answer.

A basis is

\[\left\{\left[\begin{array}{c} 1 \\ \frac{1}{2} \\ 1 \end{array}\right]\right\}.\]
