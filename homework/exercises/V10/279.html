V10 - Basis of Solution Space (ver. 279)

Find a basis for the solution space of the homogeneous system 1 x_ 1 + 1 x_ 2 + 1 x_ 3 &= 0 11 x_ 1 -9 x_ 2 + 1 x_ 3 + 5 x_ 4 &= 0 1 x_ 1 -3 x_ 2 -1 x_ 3 + 1 x_ 4 &= 0 4 x_ 1 -8 x_ 2 -2 x_ 3 + 3 x_ 4 &= 0

Answer.

A basis is

\[\left\{\left[\begin{array}{c} -\frac{1}{2} \\ -\frac{1}{2} \\ 1 \\ 0 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{c} -\frac{1}{4} \\ \frac{1}{4} \\ 0 \\ 1 \end{array}\right]\right\}.\]
