Find a basis for the solution space of the homogeneous system -1 x_ 1 + 1 x_ 3 -3 x_ 4 + 1 x_ 5 &= 0 -3 x_ 1 -1 x_ 2 + 1 x_ 3 -6 x_ 4 + 3 x_ 5 &= 0 1 x_ 1 -5 x_ 2 -2 x_ 3 + 18 x_ 4 -1 x_ 5 &= 0 -5 x_ 1 -4 x_ 2 -6 x_ 3 -3 x_ 4 + 5 x_ 5 &= 0

Answer.

A basis is

\[\left\{\left[\begin{array}{c} -3 \\ 3 \\ 0 \\ 1 \\ 0 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{c} 1 \\ 0 \\ 0 \\ 0 \\ 1 \end{array}\right]\right\}.\]
