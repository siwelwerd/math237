Find a basis for the solution space of the homogeneous system -10 x_ 1 + 5 x_ 2 + 5 x_ 3 &= 0 -10 x_ 1 + 4 x_ 2 + 2 x_ 3 &= 0 17 x_ 1 -4 x_ 2 + 5 x_ 3 &= 0 -1 x_ 1 -1 x_ 3 &= 0

Answer.

A basis is

\[\left\{\left[\begin{array}{c} -1 \\ -3 \\ 1 \end{array}\right]\right\}.\]
