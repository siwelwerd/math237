V10 - Basis of Solution Space (ver. 978)

Find a basis for the solution space of the homogeneous system 1 x_ 1 -2 x_ 2 -7 x_ 3 + 2 x_ 4 &= 0 4 x_ 1 -3 x_ 2 -14 x_ 3 + 1 x_ 4 &= 0 5 x_ 1 -2 x_ 2 -19 x_ 3 + 2 x_ 4 &= 0 -4 x_ 2 -10 x_ 3 + 5 x_ 4 &= 0

Answer.

A basis is

\[\left\{\left[\begin{array}{c} \frac{3}{2} \\ 0 \\ \frac{1}{2} \\ 1 \end{array}\right]\right\}.\]
