V10 - Basis of Solution Space (ver. 169)

Find a basis for the solution space of the homogeneous system 4 x_ 1 -5 x_ 2 + 1 x_ 3 + 3 x_ 4 &= 0 + 2 x_ 2 + 2 x_ 3 -2 x_ 4 &= 0 10 x_ 1 -4 x_ 2 + 11 x_ 3 -1 x_ 4 &= 0 6 x_ 1 + 3 x_ 2 + 12 x_ 3 -6 x_ 4 &= 0

Answer.

A basis is

\[\left\{\left[\begin{array}{c} -\frac{3}{2} \\ -1 \\ 1 \\ 0 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{c} \frac{1}{2} \\ 1 \\ 0 \\ 1 \end{array}\right]\right\}.\]
