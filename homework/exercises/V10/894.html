Find a basis for the solution space of the homogeneous system -4 x_ 1 -11 x_ 2 + 1 x_ 3 &= 0 1 x_ 1 + 5 x_ 2 -1 x_ 3 &= 0 2 x_ 1 + 1 x_ 2 + 1 x_ 3 &= 0 + 15 x_ 2 -5 x_ 3 &= 0

Answer.

A basis is

\[\left\{\left[\begin{array}{c} -\frac{2}{3} \\ \frac{1}{3} \\ 1 \end{array}\right]\right\}.\]
