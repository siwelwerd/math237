V10 - Basis of Solution Space (ver. 947)

Find a basis for the solution space of the homogeneous system 2 x_ 1 -1 x_ 2 -14 x_ 3 + 4 x_ 4 &= 0 3 x_ 1 + 4 x_ 2 + 23 x_ 3 -5 x_ 4 &= 0 8 x_ 1 + 2 x_ 2 -8 x_ 3 + 4 x_ 4 &= 0 -3 x_ 1 -2 x_ 2 -7 x_ 3 + 1 x_ 4 &= 0

Answer.

A basis is

\[\left\{\left[\begin{array}{c} 3 \\ -8 \\ 1 \\ 0 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{c} -1 \\ 2 \\ 0 \\ 1 \end{array}\right]\right\}.\]
