Find a basis for the solution space of the homogeneous system 12 x_ 1 + 5 x_ 2 -6 x_ 4 + 1 x_ 5 &= 0 -16 x_ 1 + 1 x_ 2 + 4 x_ 3 + 2 x_ 4 -6 x_ 5 &= 0 15 x_ 1 + 5 x_ 2 -5 x_ 3 -6 x_ 5 &= 0 -5 x_ 1 -4 x_ 2 + 1 x_ 3 + 1 x_ 4 + 2 x_ 5 &= 0

Answer.

A basis is

\[\left\{\left[\begin{array}{c} \frac{1}{2} \\ 0 \\ \frac{3}{2} \\ 1 \\ 0 \end{array}\right]\right\}.\]
