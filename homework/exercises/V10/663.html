Find a basis for the solution space of the homogeneous system -8 x_ 1 -3 x_ 2 -4 x_ 3 -5 x_ 4 &= 0 6 x_ 1 -6 x_ 2 + 3 x_ 3 -5 x_ 4 &= 0 -12 x_ 1 -5 x_ 2 -6 x_ 3 -2 x_ 4 &= 0 8 x_ 1 + 3 x_ 2 + 4 x_ 3 -5 x_ 4 &= 0

Answer.

A basis is

\[\left\{\left[\begin{array}{c} -\frac{1}{2} \\ 0 \\ 1 \\ 0 \end{array}\right]\right\}.\]
