Find a basis for the solution space of the homogeneous system 5 x_ 1 + 1 x_ 2 + 5 x_ 3 -4 x_ 4 -3 x_ 5 &= 0 -6 x_ 2 + 4 x_ 3 -1 x_ 4 + 2 x_ 5 &= 0 5 x_ 1 + 1 x_ 2 + 3 x_ 3 -6 x_ 4 -6 x_ 5 &= 0 1 x_ 1 -6 x_ 2 -4 x_ 3 -1 x_ 4 -5 x_ 5 &= 0

Answer.

A basis is

\[\left\{\left[\begin{array}{c} \frac{241}{299} \\ -\frac{37}{598} \\ -\frac{463}{598} \\ -\frac{217}{299} \\ 1 \end{array}\right]\right\}.\]
