V10 - Basis of Solution Space (ver. 65)

Find a basis for the solution space of the homogeneous system 3 x_ 1 -3 x_ 2 -4 x_ 3 + 2 x_ 4 + 5 x_ 5 &= 0 2 x_ 1 -2 x_ 3 -3 x_ 4 &= 0 -5 x_ 1 -2 x_ 2 + 1 x_ 4 + 2 x_ 5 &= 0 -5 x_ 1 + 5 x_ 2 -6 x_ 3 + 5 x_ 5 &= 0

Answer.

A basis is

\[\left\{\left[\begin{array}{c} \frac{12}{65} \\ \frac{19}{65} \\ \frac{12}{13} \\ -\frac{32}{65} \\ 1 \end{array}\right]\right\}.\]
