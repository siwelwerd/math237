Find a basis for the subspace

\[W=\mathrm{span}\ \left\{\left[\begin{array}{r} 2 \\ 0 \\ 2 \\ -3 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} -4 \\ -4 \\ -4 \\ 0 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} -2 \\ 8 \\ -2 \\ 15 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} -2 \\ -3 \\ 1 \\ -1 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 8 \\ -17 \\ 5 \\ -38 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} -8 \\ -10 \\ -2 \\ -2 \end{array}\right]\right\}.\]

Be sure to explain why your result is a basis.

Answer.

A basis of \(W\) is \(\left\{\left[\begin{array}{r} 2 \\ 0 \\ 2 \\ -3 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} -4 \\ -4 \\ -4 \\ 0 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} -2 \\ -3 \\ 1 \\ -1 \end{array}\right]\right\}\).
