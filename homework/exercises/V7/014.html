V7 - Basis Computation (ver. 14)

Find a basis for the subspace

\[W=\mathrm{span}\ \left\{\left[\begin{array}{r} -3 \\ 3 \\ -3 \\ -1 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 0 \\ -2 \\ 2 \\ 1 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} -12 \\ 6 \\ -6 \\ -1 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 30 \\ -16 \\ 16 \\ 3 \end{array}\right]\right\}.\]

Be sure to explain why your result is a basis.

Answer.

A basis of \(W\) is \(\left\{\left[\begin{array}{r} -3 \\ 3 \\ -3 \\ -1 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 0 \\ -2 \\ 2 \\ 1 \end{array}\right]\right\}\).
