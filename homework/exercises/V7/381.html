Find a basis for the subspace

\[W=\mathrm{span}\ \left\{\left[\begin{array}{r} -3 \\ -2 \\ -4 \\ 3 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} -2 \\ 1 \\ 1 \\ 2 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 13 \\ 11 \\ 21 \\ -13 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 25 \\ 19 \\ 37 \\ -25 \end{array}\right]\right\}.\]

Be sure to explain why your result is a basis.

Answer.

A basis of \(W\) is \(\left\{\left[\begin{array}{r} -3 \\ -2 \\ -4 \\ 3 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} -2 \\ 1 \\ 1 \\ 2 \end{array}\right]\right\}\).
