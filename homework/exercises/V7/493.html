Find a basis for the subspace

\[W=\mathrm{span}\ \left\{\left[\begin{array}{r} -2 \\ -2 \\ -4 \\ 2 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} -2 \\ 2 \\ -3 \\ -3 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 18 \\ 2 \\ 32 \\ 2 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 26 \\ -2 \\ 45 \\ 9 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} -64 \\ 4 \\ -111 \\ -21 \end{array}\right]\right\}.\]

Be sure to explain why your result is a basis.

Answer.

A basis of \(W\) is \(\left\{\left[\begin{array}{r} -2 \\ -2 \\ -4 \\ 2 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} -2 \\ 2 \\ -3 \\ -3 \end{array}\right]\right\}\).
