Find a basis for the subspace

\[W=\mathrm{span}\ \left\{\left[\begin{array}{r} -3 \\ 3 \\ -4 \\ -3 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} -1 \\ -1 \\ 1 \\ 2 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 14 \\ -4 \\ 7 \\ -1 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 0 \\ -4 \\ -1 \\ -4 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} -36 \\ 20 \\ -18 \\ 8 \end{array}\right]\right\}.\]

Be sure to explain why your result is a basis.

Answer.

A basis of \(W\) is \(\left\{\left[\begin{array}{r} -3 \\ 3 \\ -4 \\ -3 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} -1 \\ -1 \\ 1 \\ 2 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 0 \\ -4 \\ -1 \\ -4 \end{array}\right]\right\}\).
