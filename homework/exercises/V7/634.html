Find a basis for the subspace

\[W=\mathrm{span}\ \left\{\left[\begin{array}{r} -4 \\ -1 \\ 2 \\ -1 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 3 \\ 2 \\ 3 \\ -2 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 9 \\ 6 \\ 9 \\ -6 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 27 \\ 13 \\ 9 \\ -7 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 46 \\ 19 \\ 4 \\ -5 \end{array}\right]\right\}.\]

Be sure to explain why your result is a basis.

Answer.

A basis of \(W\) is \(\left\{\left[\begin{array}{r} -4 \\ -1 \\ 2 \\ -1 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 3 \\ 2 \\ 3 \\ -2 \end{array}\right]\right\}\).
