Find a basis for the subspace

\[W=\mathrm{span}\ \left\{\left[\begin{array}{r} -2 \\ 3 \\ 3 \\ -4 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 2 \\ -4 \\ 3 \\ 0 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} -14 \\ 26 \\ -9 \\ -8 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} -36 \\ 67 \\ -24 \\ -20 \end{array}\right]\right\}.\]

Be sure to explain why your result is a basis.

Answer.

A basis of \(W\) is \(\left\{\left[\begin{array}{r} -2 \\ 3 \\ 3 \\ -4 \end{array}\right] , \left[\begin{array}{r} 2 \\ -4 \\ 3 \\ 0 \end{array}\right]\right\}\).
